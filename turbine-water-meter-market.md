<p><p>The turbine water meter market is categorized into single nozzle and multi-nozzle types. Single nozzle meters feature one entry point for water flow, providing accurate measurement for lower flow rates, making them ideal for residential and small commercial applications. In contrast, multi-nozzle meters have multiple inlets, enabling measurement of higher flow rates and greater accuracy in industrial settings. Each type is tailored for specific applications, allowing users to select based on their flow requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
