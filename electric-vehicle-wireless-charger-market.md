<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resonance Technology</li><li>Inductive Charging Technolog</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Wireless Charger market encompasses two primary technologies: Magnetic Resonance and Inductive Charging. Magnetic Resonance Technology utilizes electromagnetic fields to transfer energy between coils, allowing for greater flexibility in alignment and distance, making it suitable for varied vehicle designs. Inductive Charging Technology operates through close physical contact between two coils, ensuring efficient power transfer but requiring precise alignment. Both technologies aim to enhance convenience and usability in electric vehicle charging, promoting wider adoption of electric vehicles.</p></p>
