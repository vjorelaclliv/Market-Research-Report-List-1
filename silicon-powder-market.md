<p><ul><li>Densified Silica Fume</li><li>Semi Densified Silica Fume</li><li>Undensified Silica Fume</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon powder market primarily consists of three types: densified silica fume, semi-densified silica fume, and undensified silica fume. Densified silica fume features a compact particle structure, enhancing flowability and reducing dust, making it ideal for concrete applications. Semi-densified silica fume offers a balance between flowability and versatility, suitable for various construction projects. Undensified silica fume, with its higher surface area, is often used for high-performance concrete and specialty applications, harnessing its superior reactivity and bonding properties.</p></p>
