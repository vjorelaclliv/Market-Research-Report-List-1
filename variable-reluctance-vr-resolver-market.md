<p><p>A Variable Reluctance (VR) Resolver is an electrical device used for determining angular position, often utilized in motion control applications. It consists of a rotor and stator, with the rotor having varying magnetic reluctance as it rotates, allowing for precise angle measurement. VR resolvers are renowned for their robustness, high accuracy, and reliability in harsh environments, making them suitable for aerospace, automotive, and industrial automation sectors.</p><p>The Variable Reluctance (VR) Resolver Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. The rising popularity of electric and hybrid vehicles is also contributing significantly to market expansion, as VR resolvers are integral in these technologies for accurate position sensing. Additionally, advancements in sensor technology and integration with IoT systems are enhancing the functionality and appeal of VR resolvers. The market is witnessing trends towards miniaturization and enhanced performance, alongside a growing emphasis on energy efficiency.
