<p><p>The rain tires market comprises three main types: full wet tires, intermediate tires, and wet weather tires. Full wet tires are designed for extreme wet conditions, featuring deep grooves that channel water away for optimal grip. Intermediate tires strike a balance for changing weather, offering moderate tread depth for both wet and drying surfaces. Wet weather tires are versatile, providing reliable performance in light rain and damp conditions, catering to a broader range of driving scenarios while maintaining safety and handling.</p></p>
