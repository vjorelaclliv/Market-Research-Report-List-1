<p><strong>The Dental Gel Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Opaque</li><li>Translucent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental gel materials are classified into two main types based on their appearance: opaque and translucent. Opaque dental gels are characterized by their solid color, providing coverage and masking underlying tooth structures, making them ideal for cavity filling and cosmetic applications. In contrast, translucent dental gels allow light to pass through, providing a more natural look and mimicking the appearance of healthy tooth enamel. These materials are commonly used in dental procedures such as whitening, sealing, and restorative treatments.</p></p>
