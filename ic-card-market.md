<p><p>The IC card market comprises various types based on structure and data exchange methods. Contact IC cards require physical contact for data transfer, while no-contact IC cards utilize radio frequency for communication. Dual interface IC cards support both methods. In terms of external data exchange, general memory cards store data without security, encrypted memory cards offer secure data storage, and CPU cards include microprocessor capabilities for complex operations. Super smart cards combine advanced processing features with enhanced security for versatile applications.</p></p>
