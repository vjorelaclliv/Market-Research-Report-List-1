<p><strong>The Surgical Drains/Wound Drainage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Drains</li><li>Active Drains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical drains and wound drainage market consists of passive and active drain types. Passive drains rely on gravity to allow fluid to escape from surgical sites, typically using capillary action. They are simpler and often more cost-effective. Active drains, on the other hand, use suction to remove fluid, providing better control over drainage and reducing the risk of infection. Both types cater to diverse surgical needs, influencing their demand and market dynamics based on efficiency and clinical outcomes.</p></p>
