<p><p>The semiconductor parameter analyzer market encompasses various types of measurement techniques, primarily Current-Voltage (IV) measurement and capacitance measurement. IV measurement evaluates the electrical characteristics of semiconductor devices by plotting current against voltage, helping assess device performance and behaviors such as threshold voltage and leakage current. Capacitance measurement, on the other hand, determines the capacitance properties of semiconductor materials and structures, providing insights into charge storage, depletion regions, and quality factors, which are crucial for developing efficient electronic components.</p></p>
