<p><strong>The Axial Grain Dryers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Plenum</li><li>Split Plenum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axial grain dryers are categorized into two main types: Single Plenum and Split Plenum. Single Plenum dryers utilize a centralized airflow system that efficiently dries grain through a single chamber, simplifying the drying process. In contrast, Split Plenum dryers feature dual airflow channels, allowing for more precise temperature and moisture control, enhancing drying efficiency and uniformity. Both types are crucial for maximizing harvest quality and reducing spoilage, appealing to a range of agricultural producers aiming for optimal grain management.</p></p>
