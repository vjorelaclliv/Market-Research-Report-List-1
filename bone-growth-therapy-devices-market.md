<p><p>Bone Growth Therapy Devices are categorized into three main types based on their approach: invasive, ultrasound, and non-invasive. Invasive devices typically require surgical intervention and direct application to the bone to facilitate healing. Ultrasound devices use high-frequency sound waves to promote bone regeneration and healing non-invasively. Non-invasive devices, on the other hand, utilize electromagnetic fields or electrical stimulation without any surgery, offering a patient-friendly alternative that encourages natural bone growth and repair. Each type aims to enhance recovery processes effectively.</p></p>
