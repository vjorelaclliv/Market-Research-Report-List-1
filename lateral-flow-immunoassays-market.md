<p><p>Lateral Flow Immunoassays (LFIAs) are simple diagnostic devices used to detect the presence of a target substance in a liquid sample without the need for specialized equipment. These assays employ capillary action to move the sample along a test strip, where specific antibodies interact with the target, resulting in a visible signal. Their ease of use, rapid results, and the wide array of applications—including infectious disease detection, pregnancy testing, and drug screenings—drive their popularity in healthcare.</p><p>The Lateral Flow Immunoassays Market is experiencing robust growth, projected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period. Key factors contributing to this growth include the rising prevalence of infectious diseases, increased acceptance of point-of-care testing, and advancements in assay technologies. Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has significantly accelerated the demand for rapid testing solutions, highlighting the need for efficient diagnostic tools. </p><p>Innovative trends such as the development of multiplex assays, integration with digital technology for enhanced data analysis, and improvements in sensitivity and specificity are also shaping the market landscape, ensuring that LFIAs remain an essential component of modern diagnostics.</p></p>
