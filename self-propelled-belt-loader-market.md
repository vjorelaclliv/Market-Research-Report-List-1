<p><p>The self-propelled belt loader market features various transmission types, including hydraulic-mechanical, hydraulic, and power transmissions. Hydraulic-mechanical transmission combines hydraulic elements with mechanical systems to provide efficient power transfer and control. Hydraulic transmission relies solely on hydraulic systems for smooth operation and precise control, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, power transmission encompasses a broader range of mechanisms, including mechanical gears and belts, to convert engine power into motion, catering to diverse operational needs in airport and logistics environments.</p></p>
