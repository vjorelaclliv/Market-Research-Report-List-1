<p><p>The titanium dioxide sputtering target market consists of two primary types: rotatable and non-rotating targets. Rotatable targets enable continuous deposition by allowing a larger surface area to be utilized, leading to improved efficiency and reduced material waste. These targets can be rotated during the sputtering process to maintain consistent coating thickness. In contrast, non-rotating targets are fixed and are typically used for simpler applications with lower deposition demands. Each type serves specific needs in semiconductor, photovoltaic, and optical industries.</p></p>
