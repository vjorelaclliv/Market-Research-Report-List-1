<p><p>The power converter market for Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) primarily includes AC/DC converters and DC/DC converters. AC/DC converters transform alternating current from the electrical grid into direct current, providing a stable power supply for AGV systems. In contrast, DC/DC converters regulate and convert different DC voltage levels within the AGV, ensuring efficient power distribution to various components. Both types are essential for optimizing performance, enhancing energy efficiency, and extending the operational lifespan of AGVs in various industrial applications.</p></p>
