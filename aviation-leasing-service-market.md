<p><strong>The Aviation Leasing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Lease</li><li>Dry Lease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation leasing market primarily comprises two types: wet lease and dry lease. A wet lease involves leasing an aircraft along with its crew, maintenance, and insurance, offering operators a complete service for short-term needs. In contrast, a dry lease provides only the aircraft, without crew or maintenance, allowing lessees to use their own staff and services. This distinction caters to different operational requirements and cost structures, enabling airlines to optimize their fleet management and flexibility in various markets.</p></p>
