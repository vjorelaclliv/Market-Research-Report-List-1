<p><p>The UHD 4K panel market is predominantly categorized into three types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD panels utilize liquid crystal technology to provide vibrant colors; however, they often struggle with deep blacks. LED panels, an advancement of LCD, employ light-emitting diodes for backlighting, enhancing brightness and color accuracy. OLED panels use organic compounds to emit light individually, resulting in superior contrast and true blacks. Each technology offers distinct advantages, catering to different consumer preferences and viewing experiences.</p></p>
