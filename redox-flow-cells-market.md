<p><p>Redox flow cells are a type of rechargeable battery technology that stores energy in liquid electrolytes, which are separated by a membrane. This innovative design allows for the efficient storage and discharge of energy, making them suitable for large-scale energy applications, such as renewable energy integration and grid buffering. Unlike conventional batteries, redox flow cells can be easily scaled up by increasing the size of the storage tanks, allowing them to meet varying energy demands.</p><p>The Redox Flow Cells Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
