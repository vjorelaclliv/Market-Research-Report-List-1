<p><p>The pneumatic die grinders market is primarily segmented into two types: angle grinders and straight grinders. Angle grinders feature a rotating disc positioned at a right angle to the tool body, ideal for precise cutting and grinding in tight spaces. Straight grinders, on the other hand, showcase a straight barrel design, offering versatility for more extensive tasks such as deburring and polishing. Both types leverage compressed air for powerful, efficient operation, catering to various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
