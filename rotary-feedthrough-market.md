<p><p>The rotary feedthrough market is segmented into solid shaft and hollow shaft types based on design and application. Solid shaft feedthroughs provide robust performance and are commonly used in high-torque applications, ensuring stability and durability. In contrast, hollow shaft feedthroughs allow for the passage of fluids, gases, or cables through the center, making them ideal for applications requiring integrated services. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing, aerospace, and scientific research, catering to specific operational needs.</p></p>
