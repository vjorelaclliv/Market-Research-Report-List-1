<p><ul><li>IMM</li><li>Extrusion Machines</li><li>BMM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic processing machinery market encompasses various types, primarily Injection Molding Machines (IMM), Extrusion Machines, and Blow Molding Machines (BMM). IMMs are utilized to produce precise plastic parts through injection molding, allowing for complex shapes and designs. Extrusion machines continuously shape plastic by melting it and forcing it through a die, ideal for producing profiles, sheets, and films. BMMs focus on creating hollow plastic products by inflating softened plastic in a mold, commonly used for bottles and containers. Each type plays a critical role in the manufacturing process of plastic products.</p></p>
