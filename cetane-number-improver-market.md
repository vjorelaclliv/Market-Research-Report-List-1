<p><p>Cetane Number Improver (CNI) is a chemical additive used to enhance the cetane number of diesel fuel, which measures the fuel's ignition quality. A higher cetane number improves combustion efficiency, reduces engine noise, lowers emissions, and enhances overall engine performance. Common CNIs include 2-ethylhexyl nitrate (2-EHN), di-tert-butyl peroxide, and nitrates of amyl.</p><p>The Cetane Number Improver Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for cleaner and more efficient fuels, stringent environmental regulations, and the rising adoption of high-performance diesel engines. Additionally, the shift toward renewable diesel and biodiesel is creating opportunities for innovative CNI formulations.
