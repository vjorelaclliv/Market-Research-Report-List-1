<p><p>Composite testing markets are categorized into several types based on the matrix and fiber composition. Polymer Matrix Composites (PMCs) utilize polymers for the matrix, offering lightweight and versatile applications. Continuous Fiber Composites (CFCs) feature long fibers, enhancing strength and stiffness. Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMCs) combine ceramics with fibers for high-temperature resistance. Discontinuous Fiber Composites (DFCs) use shorter fibers, providing cost-effective solutions with decent performance. The "Others" category includes emerging materials and novel composites that don't fit traditional classifications.</p></p>
