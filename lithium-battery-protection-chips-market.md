<p><ul><li>OV</li><li>OT</li><li>OCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithium battery protection chips are essential components that enhance the safety and longevity of lithium-ion batteries. The market can be categorized into three main types:</p><p>1. **OV (Over Voltage) Protection**: Prevents battery damage by cutting off charging once the voltage exceeds a specified threshold.</p><p>2. **OT (Over Temperature) Protection**: Monitors the battery's temperature and disconnects it if overheating occurs.</p><p>3. **OCD (Over Current Discharge) Protection**: Safeguards against excessive current draw, ensuring the battery operates within safe limits during discharge.</p></p>
