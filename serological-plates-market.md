<p><p>The serological plates market features distinct types based on well shapes: V Bottom, U Bottom, and Flat Bottom. V Bottom plates, with their angled sides, facilitate efficient sample recovery by directing liquids toward the bottom. U Bottom plates maintain a rounded base, promoting even mixing and minimizing dead volume, ideal for cell cultures. Flat Bottom plates offer a stable surface for assays and are commonly used in enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) due to their uniformity for optical readings. Each type serves specific laboratory needs.</p></p>
