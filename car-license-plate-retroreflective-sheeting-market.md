<p><ul><li>Prismatic Reflective Sheeting</li><li>Glass Bead Reflective Sheeting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car license plate retroreflective sheeting market primarily consists of two types: prismatic reflective sheeting and glass bead reflective sheeting. Prismatic reflective sheeting employs a micro-prismatic technology that enhances visibility by reflecting light at various angles, resulting in superior brightness and clarity. In contrast, glass bead reflective sheeting utilizes embedded glass beads to reflect light back towards its source, offering a cost-effective solution with moderate visibility. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and compliance in vehicle identification.</p></p>
