<p><p>The fully automatic thermoforming machines market encompasses various types, including vacuum forming, pneumatic thermoforming, and other specialized methods. Vacuum forming uses a vacuum to shape plastic sheets over molds, allowing for intricate designs and lightweight products. Pneumatic thermoforming employs air pressure for shaping, enabling rapid production with high precision. Other methods may include hybrid systems or unique techniques to cater to specific industry needs. Each type offers unique advantages in efficiency, complexity, and product versatility across diverse applications.</p></p>
