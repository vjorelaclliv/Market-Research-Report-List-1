<p><p>Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) ventilators are classified into two main types. Fixed Pressure BiPAP devices deliver a set inhalation and exhalation pressure, providing consistent support for patients with stable conditions. In contrast, Auto Adjusting BiPAP devices automatically adjust the pressure levels based on the patient's breathing patterns, offering personalized treatment for varying needs. Both types are essential in managing respiratory conditions, effectively improving patient comfort and ensuring adequate ventilation during sleep or medical care.</p></p>
