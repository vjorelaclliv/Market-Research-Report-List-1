<p><ul><li>Contact Type (Mechanical)</li><li>Non-contact (Ultrasonic)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tank liquid level indicators are essential for monitoring liquid levels in various industries. The contact type, or mechanical indicators, utilize direct physical interaction with the liquid to measure levels, often employing floats or gauges. Non-contact indicators, such as ultrasonic sensors, use sound waves to assess liquid levels without physical contact, offering enhanced safety and reducing wear. Each type serves distinct applications: mechanical indicators are often simpler and more cost-effective, while ultrasonic systems provide higher accuracy and are suitable for harsh environments.</p></p>
