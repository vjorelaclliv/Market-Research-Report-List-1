<p><p>The E-Scooter Helmet market is segmented into two main types: Full Face Helmets and Open Face Helmets. Full Face Helmets provide comprehensive protection as they cover the entire head, including the chin and face, making them ideal for high-speed rides and enhanced safety. In contrast, Open Face Helmets offer greater ventilation and comfort, leaving the face exposed while still protecting the skull.
