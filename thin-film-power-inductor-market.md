<p><strong>The Thin-film Power Inductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount Technology</li><li>Through Hole Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thin-film power inductor market is primarily categorized into two types: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). SMT inductors are designed for compact, high-density applications, allowing for easy integration onto printed circuit boards, making them ideal for consumer electronics and telecommunications. In contrast, THT inductors, which are inserted into pre-drilled holes on PCB, provide robustness and are often used in power supplies and industrial applications. Both types cater to specific application needs and design considerations.</p></p>
