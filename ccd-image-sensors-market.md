<p><strong>The CCD Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Full Frame Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Interline Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Frame Interline Transfer CCD Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCD image sensors market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Frame Transfer CCD sensors capture images by first storing them in an integrated area for readout, enhancing speed. Full Frame Transfer CCD sensors read the entire frame simultaneously, ideal for high-quality imaging. Interline Transfer CCD sensors utilize micro-lenses to minimize light loss during image capture, improving sensitivity. Frame Interline Transfer CCD sensors combine advantages of both, offering high efficiency and speed, making them suitable for professional photography and scientific applications.</p></p>
