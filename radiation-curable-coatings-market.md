<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet Curing</li><li>Electron Beam Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation-curable coatings market is primarily divided into two types: ultraviolet (UV) curing and electron beam (EB) curing. UV curing utilizes ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, offering rapid curing and low emissions, making it ideal for applications such as printing, automotive, and wood coatings. In contrast, electron beam curing employs high-energy electron beams for crosslinking coatings, providing deeper penetration and faster curing times, with a focus on industrial applications like electronics and packaging. Both types enhance durability and performance while reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
