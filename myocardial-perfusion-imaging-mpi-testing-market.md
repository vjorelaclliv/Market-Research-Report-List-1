<p><ul><li>Stress Myocardial Perfusion Imaging (MPI)</li><li>Resting Myocardial Perfusion Imaging (MPI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Myocardial Perfusion Imaging (MPI) testing encompasses two primary market types: Stress MPI and Resting MPI. Stress MPI evaluates blood flow to the heart during physical or pharmacological stress, revealing potential ischemic areas. This method helps assess coronary artery disease and heart function under exertion. Conversely, Resting MPI measures blood flow at rest, providing insights into the heart's status without the influence of physical activity. Together, these tests facilitate comprehensive cardiac assessments, aiding in diagnosis and management of cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
