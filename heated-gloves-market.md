<p><p>The heated gloves market primarily includes two types: chemical heated gloves and electric heated gloves. Chemical heated gloves utilize exothermic reactions within chemical pouches to generate heat, providing warmth for a limited duration, ideal for activities like skiing or outdoor work. In contrast, electric heated gloves incorporate battery-powered heating elements, offering adjustable temperatures and prolonged warmth. Both types cater to various consumer needs, enhancing comfort and functionality in cold environments, making them popular across diverse outdoor and industrial applications.</p></p>
