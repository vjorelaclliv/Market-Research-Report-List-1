<p><p>The asthma therapeutics market is divided into three primary types: inhalation, parenteral, and oral. Inhalation therapies, such as metered-dose inhalers and nebulizers, deliver medication directly to the lungs, providing rapid relief. Parenteral treatments include injections or infusions, often used for severe cases or when quick systemic effects are needed. Oral medications, available in tablet or liquid form, are convenient for chronic management but may take longer to show effects. Each type addresses different patient needs and treatment goals.</p></p>
