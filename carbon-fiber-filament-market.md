<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Filament market includes two primary types: Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. Injection Molding involves melting carbon fiber-reinforced thermoplastics and injecting them into molds to create complex, high-strength parts with precision. Extrusion Molding, on the other hand, pushes the melted material through a die to form continuous shapes like sheets, rods, or tubes, ideal for simpler, uniform products. Both methods leverage carbon fiber's lightweight and durable properties, catering to industries like automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods.</p></p>
