<p><strong>The Mountain Bike Suspension Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Spring</li><li>Coil Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mountain bike suspension spring market primarily features two types: air springs and coil springs. Air springs use compressed air for adjustable stiffness and lightweight performance, appealing to riders who value customization and reduced weight. Coil springs, on the other hand, are made from metal coils, offering consistent performance and durability under various conditions. They typically provide a more predictable feel and are favored by those seeking reliability. Each type caters to different riding styles and preferences, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
