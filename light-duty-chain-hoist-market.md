<p><p>The light-duty chain hoist market primarily comprises two types: manual and electric chain hoists. Manual chain hoists rely on human effort for operation, using a hand chain to lift and lower loads, making them cost-effective and portable for smaller tasks. Electric chain hoists, on the other hand, utilize electric power for effortless lifting, ideal for heavier loads and repetitive tasks. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring efficient material handling solutions tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
