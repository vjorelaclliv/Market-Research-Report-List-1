<p><p>Alkyd resin coatings are categorized based on resin concentration levels: low, medium, and high. Medium concentration ranges between 30% to 50%, balancing performance and affordability, making them suitable for various applications. High concentration coatings, exceeding 50%, provide exceptional durability and finish quality, ideal for industrial and high-performance uses. Each type serves distinct market needs, from economical solutions to premium products requiring superior performance.</p></p>
