<p><p>The humate fertilizers market comprises two primary types: solid and liquid. Solid humate fertilizers are typically granules or powders that provide essential nutrients through slow-release formulations, enhancing soil structure and promoting healthy root development. In contrast, liquid humate fertilizers offer a more immediate nutrient supply, allowing for quicker absorption by plants, often used in foliar applications or soil drenching. Both types improve soil fertility and microbial activity, making them valuable for sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
