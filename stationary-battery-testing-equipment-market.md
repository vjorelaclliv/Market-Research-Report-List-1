<p><p>The stationary battery testing equipment market is categorized into three main types: cell testing, module testing, and pack testing. Cell testing focuses on evaluating individual battery cells for performance and safety, ensuring they meet specified standards. Module testing assesses groups of cells assembled into modules, checking for consistency and efficiency. Pack testing involves evaluating complete battery packs, verifying overall functionality and reliability for applications in energy storage systems, electric vehicles, and grid services, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
