<p><p>The semiconductor PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment market is categorized into anode and cathode PECVD systems. Anode PECVD systems utilize an anode to generate plasma, typically offering higher deposition rates and uniformity, making them suitable for specific applications. Conversely, cathode PECVD systems, which involve a cathode as the main electrode, excel in producing thin films with excellent quality and precision, often preferred for advanced semiconductor manufacturing and intricate device fabrication processes. Both types are vital for modern electronic components.</p></p>
