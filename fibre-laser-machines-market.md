<p><strong>The Fibre Laser Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Fibre Laser</li><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fibre Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fibre laser machines market is primarily segmented into two types: Pulsed Fibre Lasers and Continuous Wave (CW) Fibre Lasers. Pulsed Fibre Lasers emit short bursts of energy, making them ideal for precise applications like engraving and micro-machining. In contrast, Continuous Wave Fibre Lasers deliver a continuous stream of light, suitable for cutting and welding thick materials with high efficiency. Both types leverage the advantages of fibre technology, including compact design, high beam quality, and improved thermal management.</p></p>
