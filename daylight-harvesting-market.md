<p><ul><li>Closed Loop</li><li>Partial Open Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Daylight harvesting refers to the use of natural light to reduce artificial lighting needs in buildings. In a closed loop market, systems operate independently, adjusting artificial lighting based on preset daylight levels, ensuring energy efficiency and consistent brightness. Partial open loop markets allow manual input or feedback from users to optimize light usage, integrating both natural and artificial sources. In an open loop market, systems fully rely on daylight sensing without user intervention, automating light adjustment for maximum energy savings.</p></p>
