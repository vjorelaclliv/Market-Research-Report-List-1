<p><p>The petrochemical catalyst market includes various types, each serving specific refining processes. Fluid Catalytic Cracking (FCC) catalysts enhance gasoline production from crude oil. Hydrotreating catalysts remove impurities, improving the quality of petroleum products. Hydrocracking catalysts facilitate heavier feedstock conversion to lighter products. Polymerization catalysts aid in producing polymers from monomers. Alkylation catalysts combine light hydrocarbons into gasoline components, while reforming catalysts convert naphtha into high-octane fuels. Other catalysts in this market also support specialized petrochemical processes.</p></p>
